SnakeSweeper

Welcome to SnakeSweeper! This is combination of two classic games: Snake and Minesweeper.

The Basics:

  • Move the snake around the board to collect apples.
  • Collecting apples reveals tiles and the amounnt of apples surrounding them.
  • As more tiles get revealed, flag the tiles you think have an apple.
  • The game ends if you collect all the apples, run into yourself, or flag and try to collect an incorrect tile.
